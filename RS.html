Indirect Speech (คำพูดรายงาน)

การเปลี่ยนแปลง Tense

Direct Speech Indirect Speech ตัวอย่าง
Present Simple Past Simple "I am exhausted." → He said (that) he was exhausted.
Present Continuous Past Continuous "I am studying." → She told me (that) she was studying.
Past Simple Past Perfect "I loved the concert." → He told me (that) he had loved the concert.
Present Perfect Past Perfect "I have never been to Rome." → She said (that) she had never been to Rome.
Will Would "I will help you." → He said (that) he would help me.
Must Had to "You must pay attention." → She told me (that) I had to pay attention.

หมายเหตุสำคัญ:

  • คำว่า "that" สามารถละได้ในประโยครายงาน
  • ต้องเปลี่ยนสรรพนาม (I, you, he, she, etc.) ให้เหมาะสม
  • คำแสดงเวลาและสถานที่ต้องเปลี่ยนให้สอดคล้อง:
    • now → then
    • today → that day
    • tomorrow → the next day
    • yesterday → the day before
    • this → that
    • here → there

ตัวอย่างการเปลี่ยนคำพูดรายงานทั้งประโยค:

Direct: "I am going to Paris next week."

Indirect: She said (that) she was going to Paris the following week.

Direct: "Where do you live?"

Indirect: He asked me where I lived.

Direct: "I can't come to the party."

Indirect: She told me (that) she couldn't come to the party.
