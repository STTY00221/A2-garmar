การใช้ To-infinitive และ For

1. การใช้ To-infinitive แสดงจุดประสงค์ (Purpose)

โครงสร้าง: Verb + to + infinitive

  • I went to Madrid to visit some family.
  • I need more time to study for the exam.
2. การใช้ For + Noun

โครงสร้าง: For + Noun

  • I went to the shop for some milk.
  • These gloves are used for protection.

❌ ข้อผิดพลาดที่พบบ่อย:

I went to the shop for buying some milk.

ที่ถูกต้อง: I went to the shop to buy some milk.

การใช้ -ing Form

1. การใช้ -ing Form เป็นประธาน
  • Cheating is not allowed.
  • Eating candy is bad for your teeth.
2. การใช้ -ing Form หลัง Preposition
  • I'm tired of waiting for you.
  • He's very good at painting.
3. Verbs + -ing Form

กริยาที่ตามด้วย -ing form:

avoid, enjoy, finish, hate, keep, like, love, mind, prefer, recommend, spend time, stop, suggest

  • I don't mind waiting.
  • She recommended eating here.

การใช้ Bare Infinitive

1. หลัง do, does, did
  • He didn't say anything.
  • Does Tim work with you?
2. หลัง Modal Verbs
  • You should come with us.
  • I can't play the guitar.

กรณีพิเศษและการใช้ในรูปปฏิเสธ

1. การใช้ Not

To-infinitive: Not + to + infinitive

  • She decided not to enter the competition.
  • I promise not to lie again.

-ing form: Not + verb-ing

  • I love not having to go to work.
  • I hate not hearing from you.
